\subsection{Arrow Widget} \begin{figure}[ht] \hbox to \columnwidth{\hfil \epsffile{figures/Arrow.eps} \hfil} \caption{Arrow Widget Design} \label{fig:ArrowDesign} \end{figure} The Arrow Widget consists of a sphere with a cylinder extending from it as shown in figure~\ref{fig:ArrowDesign}. At the end of the cylinder is a cone. The primary use of the Arrow Widget is for specifying the seedpoint of an isosurface growing algorithm, where the arrow will be pointed along the normal to the isosurface at that point and the length of the arrow could indicate curvature of the isosurface. (Note: the Arrow Widget requires no constraints.) The user moves the Arrow Widget by dragging any of its graphical components.
